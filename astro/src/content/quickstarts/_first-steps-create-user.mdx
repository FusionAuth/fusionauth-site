## Create a New User

Point your browser at http://localhost:9011/admin

From this dashboard, click on "Users" in the left hand navigation bar.

![Dashboard](/img/quickstarts/dashboard-users-selected.png)

Click the green add button (+) at the top right of the page.

Fill in the form per the image below.

- Email: fred@example.com
- Username: fred
- Deselect the button to create passwords by email
- Enter 'password' as the password

Click the blue save button at the top right of the screen.

![Dashboard](/img/quickstarts/add-user.png)
