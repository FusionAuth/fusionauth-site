Welcome to FusionAuth `1.65.0`; the world may never be the same. This version includes Tenant Manager SSO, and more. As you might expect, we've also fixed a wide variety of bugs as part of our Spring cleaning.

We're calling this release TMS^2: the Tenant Manager SSO Tasmanian Mountain Skink, since this version enables self-service SSO configuration for enterprise users in a manner not unlike the territoriality of a [tasmanian mountain skink](https://nre.tas.gov.au/wildlife-management/fauna-of-tasmania/reptiles-and-frogs/tasmanian-lizards/mountain-skink):
