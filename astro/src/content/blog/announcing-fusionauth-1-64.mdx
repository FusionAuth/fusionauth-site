We're excited to release FusionAuth 1.64.0. This version includes [Lambda Secrets](/docs/extend/code/lambdas#using-secrets), an improvement to OAuth2 metadata to help dynamic discovery of FusionAuth OAuth endpoints, the ability to use codes for email passwordless login, and custom page titles for custom simple themes. As usual, we've also taken the time to quash some longstanding bugs.

We're calling this release the Secret Shibe since this version stores secret values for your lambdas as assuredly as a faithful doggo stores rawhide bones in your couch cushions:
