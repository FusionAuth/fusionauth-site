The length of the secure generator used for generating the {props.generator_display_name}.

If the <InlineField>{props.generator_type}.type</InlineField> is equal to <InlineFieldValue>randomAlpha</InlineFieldValue> then the length must be greater or equal to 4 and less than or equal to 12.
If the <InlineField>{props.generator_type}.type</InlineField> is equal to <InlineFieldValue>randomAlphaNumeric</InlineFieldValue> then the length must be greater or equal to 4 and less than or equal to 12.
If the <InlineField>{props.generator_type}.type</InlineField> is equal to <InlineFieldValue>randomBytes</InlineFieldValue> then the length must be greater or equal to 16 and less than or equal to 128.
If the <InlineField>{props.generator_type}.type</InlineField> is equal to <InlineFieldValue>randomDigits</InlineFieldValue> then the length must be greater or equal to 4 and less than or equal to 12.
