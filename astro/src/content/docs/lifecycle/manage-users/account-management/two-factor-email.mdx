## Add a Two Factor Method - Email

Email two factor is not enabled by default. Configure your tenant by navigating to <strong>Tenants -> Edit Tenant -> Multi-Factor</strong>.

### Enable MFA method on Tenant (Admin Facing)

<img src="/img/docs/lifecycle/manage-users/account-management/enable-email-tenant.png" alt="Configure Tenant Email"  width="1200" />

You can enable SMS MFA on the tenant level by following these steps:

- Toggle this MFA method by clicking `enabled`
- Select a two factor authentication template (FusionAuth ships with a default to be customized if needed)

### Enable Email Factor from Account Management (User Facing)

<img src="/img/docs/lifecycle/manage-users/account-management/account-management-index-crop.png" alt="Account Management Index"  width="1200"  role="bottom-cropped" />

1. Navigate back to your account page.
2. Click `Manage two-factor`
3. Click `Add two-factor`
4. There will be an option for Email.

<img src="/img/docs/lifecycle/manage-users/account-management/add-factors-home-crop.png" alt="Add Factors"  width="1200"  role="bottom-cropped" />
<img src="/img/docs/lifecycle/manage-users/account-management/email-configure-cropped.png" alt="Add Email 2FA"  width="1200"  role="bottom-cropped" />

Next,

1. Enter your email address.
2. Click on  `Send a one-time-code`
3. Enter the `Verification Code`
4. Click `Enable`.

### Recovery Codes (User Facing)

Now you will be presented with recovery codes. Save these in a safe space.

<img src="/img/docs/lifecycle/manage-users/account-management/mfa-recovery-codes-crop.png" alt="Recovery Codes"  width="1200"  role="bottom-cropped" />

<Icon name="trophy" /> Success!

Upon the next login, you will be prompted for an emailed code in addition to your password.

### See It in Action (User Facing)

With email MFA enabled, if you log out and log back in you will be presented with the following screen in addition to the typical login screen.

<img src="/img/docs/lifecycle/manage-users/account-management/auth-challenge-account-screen-crop.png" alt="Challenge Account Management"  width="1200"  role="bottom-cropped" />
