As a flexible developer tool, FusionAuth can be used in many different ways. We've even heard of customers using us for localized email template management. But there are a few key use cases that FusionAuth particularly shines at, and this section outlines them.

* [Auth as a service](/docs/get-started/use-cases/auth-service)
* [App suite](/docs/get-started/use-cases/app-suite)
* [Business to business to consumer (b2b2c)](/docs/get-started/use-cases/b2b2c)
* [Business to business to employee (b2b2e)](/docs/get-started/use-cases/b2b2e)
* [Machine to machine communication (m2m)](/docs/get-started/use-cases/m2m)
* [Identity broker](/docs/get-started/use-cases/identity-proxy) byo IDP for customers
* [Authorization hub for social](/docs/get-started/use-cases/authorization-hub)
