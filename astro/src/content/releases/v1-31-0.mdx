<Item category="known-issue" viaIssue="1517" resolvedIn="1.32.1">

</Item>

Allowed modification or fabrication of an email or username in the Identity Provider Reconcile Lambda regardless of Identity Provider type.
</Item>

</Item>

<Item category="fix" issue="1171">

</Item>

</Item>

<Item category="fix" issue="1449">

</Item>

<Item category="fix" issue="1452" thanks="johnmaia" since="1.30.2">

</Item>

<Item category="fix" issue="1470">

<Item category="fix" issue="1473" since="1.30.2">
